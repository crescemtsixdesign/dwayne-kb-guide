Squat Fixes

Use this when squat mechanics feel ugly or your shins start talking. Clean reps first. Load later.

Read this straight: mild leg stiffness after a new KB session is normal. Front shin pain in goblet squats is a sign to regress the movement, not force through it.

5-minute pre-squat reset

1) Ankle rocks
10 reps/side
Get the ankle moving before you ask for a deep squat. Helps if your heels pop up or you feel stuck at the bottom.
Watch on YouTube
2) Bodyweight box squat
2 x 5 slow reps
Use a chair or box. Sit back, stay tall, and stop before pain shows up. This is your form reset.
Watch on YouTube
3) Hip hinge drill
10 reps
Groove the hinge so your squat doesn’t turn into a knee-dominant mess. Also helps if you’re mixing up squat and hinge patterns.
Watch on YouTube
4) Paused goblet squat to box
1-2 light sets x 5
Last step before loaded squats. Light bell, controlled descent, brief pause, no pain chase.
Watch on YouTube

Squat cues

Do thisNot this
  • Feet about shoulder width
  • Toes slightly out
  • Whole foot planted
  • Chest tall
  • Hold the kettlebell close
  • Think sit back and down
  • Stop at the deepest pain-free depth
  • Knees shooting way forward
  • Heels lifting
  • Torso collapsing forward
  • Forcing depth just to look good
  • Pushing through sharp or pinpoint pain
